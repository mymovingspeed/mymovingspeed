About My Moving Speed

Your reliable companion for real-time speed tracking, powered by native GPS technology.

Our Mission

My Moving Speed was born from a simple need: a fast, accurate, and privacy-focused way to check movement speed without installing bulky, ad-ridden applications. Whether you're testing your car's speedometer, tracking a bicycle ride, or hitting a new personal record while running, our goal is to provide data you can trust.

Why Choose Us?

  • Privacy First: We don't store your location data. All calculations happen directly on your device.
  • No External APIs: By using native browser Geolocation, we ensure the fastest possible response time.
  • Multi-Device: Works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and even laptops with GPS capabilities.
  • Minimalist Design: A clean, high-contrast interface designed for maximum legibility in outdoor conditions.

The Technology

We leverage the latest Web APIs to communicate directly with your device's GPS hardware. This allows us to provide sub-meter accuracy and real-time updates as you move, converting raw coordinates into easy-to-read speed metrics in km/h, mph, or m/s.
